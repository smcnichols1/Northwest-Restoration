About Northwest Restoration Co.

Northwest Restoration Co. is fully insured and bonded. Our employees are skilled members of the Bricklayers and Allied Crafts Union with many years of experience.

I have been employed in the masonry restoration industry for over 40 years. My experience includes all phases of masonry restoration, from field work to field supervision, estimating and management.

Northwest Restoration Co. specializes in masonry and concrete restoration including historical restoration projects.
